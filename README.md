# Hi, I'm Julia 👋

I'm a Principal Developer Relations Engineer at [Dash0](https://dash0.com), where I help developers adopt OpenTelemetry and build practical observability workflows. I'm also one of the community managers for the [OpenTelemetry project](https://opentelemetry.io).

I started my career as a lawyer in Brazil, studied business at UC Berkeley, worked in marketing, and pivoted into tech in 2022. Honestly, the non-linear path turned out to be the feature, not a bug. Every step gave me something I use every day.

Since then I've been active in open source across the Kubernetes docs, OpenTelemetry docs, and CNCF glossary, and I now serve as a community manager for the OpenTelemetry project. I'm always learning. That's kind of the whole point.

---

## What I'm up to

- 🔭 Working on OpenTelemetry adoption and observability content at [Dash0](https://dash0.com)
- 🌱 Going deep on OpenTelemetry, distributed tracing, and observability. Learning in public whenever I can
- 🎤 [Speaking at conferences](https://github.com/juliafmorgado/talks) worldwide (in four languages 😅)
- 🤝 Organizing [Kubernetes Community Days NY](https://community.cncf.io/kubernetes-community-days/), [AWS Community Day New York](https://www.awscommunity.us/new-york), and [CNCF Meetup NYC](https://community.cncf.io/new-york/)
- 💬 Ask me about getting into tech from a non-traditional background, open source contribution, or getting started with OpenTelemetry
